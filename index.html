About Me

I am a first-year Master student at Cornell Tech. I received my B.E degree in Software Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2024. Previously, I worked with Prof. Hanspeter Pfister at Harvard University Vision Computing Group on diffusion-based image editing and video understanding. I was also a student intern at System Intelligence Group, Microsoft Research Asia working on LLM reasoning supervised by Dr. Xueting Han. Currently, I am collaborating with Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang on 3D/4D editing tasks.

As a (not that professional) video creator, I think images and videos are fun! My research interest is enhancing multi-modal models' ability on visual reasoning tasks, such as image/video editing and vision foundation models. Besides, I'd love for you to subscribe to my Bilibili channel!
